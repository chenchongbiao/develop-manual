◆ dxpLineWidth

U16 wvWare::Word95::BRC::dxpLineWidth

When dxpLineWidth is 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5, this field is the width of a single line of border in units of 0.75 points.Each line in the border is this wide (e.g. a double border is three lines).Must be nonzero when brcType is nonzero.When dxpLineWidth is 6, it means that the border line is dotted.When dxpLineWidth is 7, it means the border line is dashed.
