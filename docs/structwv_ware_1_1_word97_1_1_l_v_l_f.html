◆ rgbxchNums

U8 wvWare::Word97::LVLF::rgbxchNums[9]

contains the character offsets into the LVL's XST of the inherited numbers of previous levels. This array should be zero terminated unless it is full (all 9 levels full). The XST contains place holders for any paragraph numbers contained in the text of the number, and the place holder contains the ilvl of the inherited number, so lvl.xst[lvl.rgbxchNums[0]] == the level of the first inherited number in this level.
