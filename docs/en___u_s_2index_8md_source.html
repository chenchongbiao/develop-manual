3 ## Overview|../common/icon_overview.svg|
4 
5 Deepin Music is a local music playing application with brand new UI design and extreme playing experience developed by Deepin Technology. It's featured with local music scanning and lyric sync functions, etc.
6 
7 ## Guide|../common/icon_guide.svg|
8 
9 You can run, close and create a shortcut for Deepin Music by the following ways.
10 
11 ### Run Deepin Music
12 
13 1. Click on ![launcher_icon](icon/launcher_icon.svg) or move the mouse pointer to the upper left corner of the screen to enter Launcher interface.
14 2. Locate ![music_icon-24](icon/music_icon-24.svg) by scrolling mouse wheel or searching by "deepin music" in Launcher interface.
15 3. Right click on the Deepin Music application icon, you can
16  - Click on **Send to desktop** to create a desktop shortcut.
17  - Click on **Send to dock** to fix the application on dock.
18  - Click on **Add to startup** to add the application to startup, it will automatically run when the system starts up.
19 
20 > ![notes](icon/notes.svg): If Deepin Music has been fixed on Dock, you can also click on its icon to run.
21 
22 ### Minimize to System Tray
23 
24 1. On Deepin Music interface, click on ![close](icon/close_icon.svg), select **Minimize to system tray**.
25 2. Deepin Music will be minimized to tray;
26 3. Right click on the Deepin Music application icon in the tray, you can:
27  - Click on **Play/Pause** to play/pause the song;
28  - Click on **Previous** to play previous song;
29  - Click on **Next** to play next song.
30  - Click on **Exit** to exit Deepin Music.
31 
32 
33 ![0|ask](png/ask.png)
34 
35 
36 ### Exit Deepin Music
37 
38 - On Deepin Music interface, click on ![close](icon/close_icon.svg), and select **Exit** to exit Deepin Music.
39 - Right click on the Deepin Music application icon on Dock, select **Close All** to exit Music.
40 - Click on ![Settings](icon/icon_menu.svg) and select **Exit** to exit Deepin Music.

110 ## Common Operations|../common/icon_commoncoperat.svg|
111 
112 You can add, delete and view info of the local music in Deepin Music.
113 
114 ### Add File
115 
116 On Deepin Music interface, you can:
117 - Click on **Add folder** to add a music folder.
118 - Click on **Scan** to scan music directory.
119 - Click on ![menu](icon/icon_menu.svg) > **Add folder** to add a music folder.
120 - Click on ![menu](icon/icon_menu.svg) > **Add file** to add a music file.
121 
122 ![0|add](png/add.png)
123 
124 > ![notes](icon/notes.svg): You can also drag and drop music file to Deepin Music interface to add.
125 
126 ### Search Music
127 
128 1. At the top of the Deepin Music interface, click on **Search**.
129 2. Enter the keywords in search box, then the result will be shown on the interface and you can locate the music quickly.
130 
131 ![1|searchmusic](png/searchmusic.png)
132 
133 ### Add Music to My Favorites
134 
135 On Deepin Music interface, click on ![collect](icon/collect.svg) to add music to my favorites.
136 
137 > ![notes](icon/notes.svg): You can also right click on the music, and click on **Add to playlist** > **My Favorites** to finish this task.

147 ### Delete Music
148 
149 On Deepin Music interface, right click on the music, you can:

151 - Click on **Delete from local disk** to remove from the playlist and delete from local disk. There will be a prompt to confirm the operation in the popup window.

178

180  - Click on ![list_icon](icon/list_icon.svg), then click on **New playlist**.
181  - Click on ![menu](icon/icon_menu.svg) to select **New Playlist**.
182  - Right click on the music and click on **Add to playlist** > **New playlist**.
183 2. Enter a name of the playlist.
184 3. Click on the blank area or press **![Enter](icon/Enter.svg)** key to confirm.

229 ### Basic Settings
230 
231 1. On Deepin Music interface, click on ![menu](icon/icon_menu.svg).
232 2. Select **Settings** > **Basic settings**.
233 3. In the option **Play**, you can:
234  - Check the box of **Autoplay**, the music will be played automatically when Deepin Music runs.
235  - Check the box of **Remember playback position**, the music will be played automatically from last position played when Deepin Music runs.
236  - Check the box of **Enable fade**, the music will fade in and out when Deepin Music runs.
237 4. In the option of **Close Main Window**, you can:
238  - Select **Minimize to system tray** to minimize Deepin Music to tray.
239  - Select **Exit Deepin Music** to exit.
